About Kelly

With over a decade of experience behind the microphone, I've lent my voice to national television campaigns, animated series, audiobooks, and corporate projects for brands across the globe.

My training in classical theater and improv gives me the range to shift from a warm, conversational read to a high-energy character performance in a single session.

I've had the privilege of working with studios like Pixar, Netflix, Audible, and dozens of independent creators. Whether it's a 30-second spot or a 12-hour audiobook, I bring the same dedication and craft.
